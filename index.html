About Me

Where Engineering Precision Meets Human Transformation

I am Zilpah Idaa, a certified SAP PM Consultant with a B.Tech in Petroleum Engineering, specialising in plant maintenance systems within the oil and gas industry. My work sits at the intersection of technical excellence and operational strategy, helping organisations build proactive, world-class maintenance cultures using SAP PM.

My journey has been one of deliberate reinvention, from mastering the technical foundations of petroleum engineering to becoming a certified authority in SAP Plant Maintenance. That process taught me that the discipline required to grow professionally mirrors the discipline required to grow personally.

Beyond the boardroom and the system, I am a writer and self-development coach. Through my book "Healed by Me" and my coaching practice, I help individuals do the most important work of their lives, the inner work.

Projects & Case Studies

Structured SAP PM thinking applied to real operational challenges.

01
SAP PM

Preventive Maintenance System Design, Oil Refinery

Problem

Frequent unplanned equipment failures in rotating equipment causing costly downtime and safety risks.

Approach

Designed a full SAP PM strategy: Functional Location hierarchy, Equipment Master setup, and scheduled Task Lists for all critical assets.

SAP PMMaintenance PlanningFMEAExcel
Outcome: Estimated 40% reduction in unplanned breakdowns through structured preventive scheduling.
02
Engineering

Equipment Criticality & Maintenance Strategy Framework

Problem

No structured criticality classification, equal resources allocated to unequal risks across a production facility.

Approach

Built a risk-based matrix categorising assets as Critical, Essential and General — each tier mapped to a tailored SAP PM strategy.

Risk MatrixRCMSAP PMProcess Docs
Outcome: Enabled teams to prioritise the 20% of assets driving 80% of operational risk.
03
Operations

Work Order Efficiency Improvement, Gas Processing Plant

Problem

Recurring bottlenecks in work order processing causing delayed execution and an unmanageable maintenance backlog.

Approach

Mapped the full SAP PM work order lifecycle and identified waste using lean maintenance principles.

SAP PMLean MaintenanceProcess MappingKPI Design
Outcome: Redesigned workflow reduced average cycle time by 30%, improving wrench time significantly.
